About

Jordi Wild is a Spanish actor, youtuber and model, born in Barcelona (Spain) in the mid-1980s, known for being one of the content creators of the The most original, complete network with the most followers of the current panorama, currently adding more than 12 million of followers in the global of their networks.
For a period of more than 5 years, and before his media explosion on the YouTube platform with its channel "El Rincón de Giorgio", Jordi cultivated a wide experience in the world of acting participant as an actor in various plays throughout Spain, as well as dozens of short films, some of which they got several prizes in prestigious industry festivals.

In recent years, in addition to dedicating himself to his YouTube channel, where he has entertained millions of people every month, he has made a profitable foray into the literary world, with the publication of his first novel "Sueños de Acero y Neón", followed by from a comic called “Jorgemyte, Agent la PEM”; both great successes with several editions.
He also highlights the creation of his own brand of clothing and accessories, "Wild Style by Jordi Wild", defined by Jordi himself as:
"A project that aims to represent and dress all those people who decide to take a step to stand out from the rest and enjoy living life with personality, intensity and passion."
